- [Opdaterer DNS-posterne i Office 365](https://support.office.com/article/B0F3FDCA-8A80-4E8E-9EF3-61E8A2A9AB23)
    
- [Tilføje eller redigere brugerdefinerede DNS-poster](https://support.office.com/article/AF00A516-DD39-4EDA-AF3E-1EAF686C8DC9)
