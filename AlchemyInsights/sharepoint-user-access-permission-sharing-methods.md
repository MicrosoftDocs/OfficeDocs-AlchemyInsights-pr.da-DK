- [Dele af et websted](https://support.office.com/en-us/article/share-a-site-958771a8-d041-4eb8-b51c-afea2eae3658)

- [Ændre kædetypen standard, når brugere få links til deling](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/sharepoint/change-default-sharing-link)

- [Stoppe deling af OneDrive eller SharePoint filer eller mapper, eller ændre tilladelser](https://support.office.com/en-us/article/stop-sharing-onedrive-or-sharepoint-files-or-folders-or-change-permissions-0a36470f-d7fe-40a0-bd74-0ac6c1e13323?ui=en-US&amp;rs=en-US&amp;ad=US)

- [Deling og tilladelser](https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Sharing-and-permissions-ac85fbf1-2431-49bf-8690-f1a2b98af65f#ID0EAABAAA=Manage_permissions)
