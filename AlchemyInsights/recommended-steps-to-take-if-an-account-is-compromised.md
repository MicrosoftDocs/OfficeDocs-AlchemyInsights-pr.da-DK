1. <span data-ttu-id="b4c86-104">[Nulstil brugerens adgangskode](https://support.office.com/article/7a5d073b-7fae-4aa5-8f96-9ecd041aba9c) med det samme.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4c86-104">[Reset the user's password](https://support.office.com/article/7a5d073b-7fae-4aa5-8f96-9ecd041aba9c) immediately.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4c86-105">Skriv ikke den nye adgangskode til slutbrugeren via mail.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4c86-105">Do not communicate the new password through email to the end user.</span></span> 
    
2. <span data-ttu-id="b4c86-106">Fjern alle mistænkelige [videresendelsesmailadresser](https://support.office.com/article/ab5eb117-0f22-4fa7-a662-3a6bdb0add74), der er angivet på postkasseniveauet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4c86-106">Remove any suspicious [forwarding addresses](https://support.office.com/article/ab5eb117-0f22-4fa7-a662-3a6bdb0add74) set at the mailbox level.</span></span> 
    
3. <span data-ttu-id="b4c86-107">Fjern alle mistænkelige [indbakkeregler](https://support.office.com/article/1433E3A0-7FB0-4999-B536-50E05CB67FED), der er angivet i postkassen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4c86-107">Remove any suspicious [inbox rules](https://support.office.com/article/1433E3A0-7FB0-4999-B536-50E05CB67FED) set within the mailbox.</span></span> 
    
4. <span data-ttu-id="b4c86-108">Hvis brugeren er blevet blokeret og ikke kan sende mails, skal du [gå til Brugere med begrænset adgang for at fjerne blokeringen af kontoen](https://protection.office.com/?hash=/restrictedusers).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4c86-108">If the user is blocked from sending email, [go to the Restricted Users to unblock the account](https://protection.office.com/?hash=/restrictedusers).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4c86-109">Når du har gjort dette, bør der inden for én time igen kunne afsendes mails fra kontoen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4c86-109">Once done, you should be able to resume sending messages  within 1 hour  .</span></span>
    
5. <span data-ttu-id="b4c86-110">Fjern brugerkontoen fra alle [administratorrollegrupper](https://support.office.com/article/eac4d046-1afd-4f1a-85fc-8219c79e1504), indtil du er sikker på, at kontoen ikke længere er kompromitteret.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4c86-110">Remove the user account from any [administrative role groups](https://support.office.com/article/eac4d046-1afd-4f1a-85fc-8219c79e1504) until you are confident that the account is no longer compromised.</span></span> 
    
<span data-ttu-id="b4c86-111">For at minimere sandsynligheden for et databrud eller en kompromitteret konto i fremtiden, anbefaler vi, at du læser vores [Office 365 Security-artikel om bedste fremgangsmåder](https://support.office.com/article/9295e396-e53d-49b9-ae9b-0b5828cdedc3).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4c86-111">To minimize the potential of a data breach or a compromised account in the future, we recommend reading our [Office 365 Security best practices article](https://support.office.com/article/9295e396-e53d-49b9-ae9b-0b5828cdedc3).</span></span>
