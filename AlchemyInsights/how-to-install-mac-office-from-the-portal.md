# <a name="how-to-install-mac-office-from-the-portal"></a>Sådan installeres Office til Mac fra portalen

1. [Afinstallere Office 2011 for Mac fra dine computere](https://support.office.com/article/4bfcd230-0ea1-4656-bf30-dbfa44d358fa).
    
2. [Sikre, at Mac-programmer er aktiveret på Office 365 admin-portalen](https://support.office.com/article/c13051e6-f75c-4737-bc0d-7685dcedf360).
    
3. [Tildele en licens til Office til Mac-brugere](https://support.office.com/article/997596B5-4173-4627-B915-36ABAC6786DC).
    
4. Brugere kan derefter installere Office-software selv, eller du kan bruge [Installation af Office til Mac-brugere](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/DeployOffice/mac/deployment-guide-for-office-for-mac).
