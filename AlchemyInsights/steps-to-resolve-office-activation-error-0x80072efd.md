# <a name="steps-to-resolve-office-activation-error-0x80072efd"></a>Trin til at løse Office activation fejl 0x80072EFD


Prøv disse løsninger for at rette fejl i Office-aktivering:
  
1. Kontroller din [firewall](https://support.office.com/article/0d23d3c0-c19c-4b2f-9845-5344fedc4380#BKMK_CheckFirewall).
    
2. Kontroller din [antivirussoftware](https://support.office.com/article/0d23d3c0-c19c-4b2f-9845-5344fedc4380#BKMK_CheckAV).
    
3. Kontroller [proxy-indstillinger](https://support.office.com/article/0d23d3c0-c19c-4b2f-9845-5344fedc4380#BKMK_CheckProxy)
    
4. Hente og installere [understøttelse og genoprettelse Assistant](https://aka.ms/SARA-OfficeActivation-Alchemy) og køre **Aktivering fejlfinding**.
